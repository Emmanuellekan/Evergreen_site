EVERGREEN CARGO, a Certified agent both in the UK and Nigeria. Evergreen Cargo is an international freight forwarding company that provides primarily Air and Ocean cargo services from the United Kingdom to other Nigeria and fro, especially to customers that ship to Lagos. Over the years, Evergreen has established itself as a highly qualified and reliable air and ocean cargo service provider.
